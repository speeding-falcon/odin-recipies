Carbonara

Oh, spaghetti alla carbonara: the ever-so-popular pasta dish with a silky egg and cheese sauce, chewy pieces of cured pork, and lots of freshly cracked black pepperâ€¦

Ingredients:

  • Spaghetti
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Bacon
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Pepper

Steps:

  1. Mix the eggs, milk and cheese and gently heat until mixed
  2. Boil the spaghetti till al-dente
  3. Cut the bacon into small pieces and fry them till crispy
  4. Drain and mix the spaghetti with the sauce and bacon
  5. Add grated cheese on top
