Awadhi Mutton Biryani

Awadhi Mutton Biryani is a royal dish that can make anyoneâ€™s mouth water. People are constantly looking for the best Mutton Biryani in their city, but do you know where the secret lies?

Ingredients:

  • Mutton
  • Saffron
  • Basmati Rice
  • Spices
  • Curd
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Ghee

Steps:

  1. Fry the spices in the ghee
  2. Fry the onions, garlic and mutton(marinated with curd and chilli and salt) in the ghee
  3. Layer rice and meat in a big pot
  4. Add saffron milk
  5. Cook on low flame until cooked
