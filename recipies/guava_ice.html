Guava Ice Cream

Homemade guava ice cream with just 3 main ingredients with available gadgets at home. Creamy, flavourful and delicious ice cream with the pink guava.

Ingredients:

  • 2 Guavas
  • 1/2 Cup Cream
  • 1 tbsp Sugar
  • Chilli powder
  • Rock salt

Steps:

  1. Cut the guava and remove the seeds
  2. Blend with cream and sugar
  3. Freeze until frozen
  4. Garnish with chilli powder and rock salt
